Major Investors Driving Dholera's Development

Dholera Smart City (SIR)

Dholera Special Investment Region (SIR) in Gujarat is India's first greenfield smart city and a premier industrial node under the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC). It is rapidly developing into a global high-tech manufacturing hub, featuring world-class infrastructure like an international airport, expressway connectivity, and India’s first semiconductor fabrication unit.

  • Location & Scope: Situated near Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Dholera is a large-scale planned industrial smart city.
  • Infrastructure: Offers plug-and-play infrastructure, underground utilities, smart systems, and sustainable development.
  • Semiconductor Hub: Home to India’s first semiconductor fabrication plant led by Tata Semiconductor Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd.
  • Connectivity: Connected via expressway, upcoming metro rail, and international airport.
  • Economic Growth: Designed to boost industrial development, jobs, and foreign investment.
  • City Planning: Includes residential, commercial, industrial zones and green spaces.
